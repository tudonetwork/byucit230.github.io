Cycle the Continental Divide - 2019



The US's Biggest Cycling Event
The world’s best cycle brands present their newest and most ground-breaking bikes, kit, clothing and accessories at this annual 3-day showcase in January in Bozeman, Montana.
The National Showcase for any cyclist – everyone is welcome, whether you’re a newcomer, a weekend cyclist or a professional.
From road to mountain biking, electric bikes to BMX – if it’s got two wheels, it’s at the Cycle Show.

For nearly twenty years, the Cycle the Continental Divide has been providing people to chance to:
– See, try and buy the latest models across all makes, models and price ranges
– Discover something new on one of our bespoke Test Tracks
– Catch up on the latest industry news with key brands, professionals and industry experts
– Meet and hear from the best pro-riders on the Main Stage or as part of our exclusive professional demos, and so much more…
